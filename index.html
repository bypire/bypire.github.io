Bridge Weigh-in-Motion

Inverse problem & uncertainty

Recovering a truck's axle weights from a bridge's response, as a regularized Bayesian inverse problem: analytical verification, Tikhonov regularization of the ill-posed axle split, uncertainty calibrated from model error, and validation on real KW51 data.

Optimal Racing Line

Optimal control

The minimum-lap-time racing line computed as an optimal-control problem: arc-length direct collocation with hand-derived analytic Jacobians, solved as an NLP and verified against the closed-form skidpad optimum. The line emerges from the grip limit.

Vortex Street CFD

From-scratch Navier–Stokes

A 2D incompressible Navier–Stokes solver written from scratch (staggered MAC grid, projection method), verified against the Ghia lid-driven-cavity benchmark, then inverted into a vortex flow-meter that reads flow speed from the wake frequency.
